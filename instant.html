  • S. Podophyllum Receives the "Critique of Pure Reason"
  • 2020
  • Plant, desk lamp, microcontroller
  • 12 x 12 x 12 in

    • Table lamp modified to "blink out" the bitstream of Kant's "Critique of Pure Reason." Each character of the original text transmitted to S. podophyllum as a series of light/dark flashes, e.g.:

    H ↣ 01001000 ↣ Off/On/Off/Off/On/Off/Off/Off

    The process is repeated indefinitely. Over time, the information of the book is woven into S. podophyllum as its sole source of energy.

  • Untitled
  • 2020
  • Solenoid, twine, tape, microcontroller
  • Installation

    • Linear solenoid attached to jute twine. The number of threads is the maximum that the solenoid has the force to pull. With each pulse, the solenoid tensions the twine, causing it to resonate briefly before being released.

  • get_dist
  • 2020
  • Thermal printer, distance sensor, microcontroller
  • 12 x 12 x 36 in

    • System that measures and prints its own distance from the nearest wall on a looped roll of thermal paper. Eventually, due to repeated printing, the data dissolves into a solid black line.
